# Related Work

## Quantum Data Structures and Fingerprinting
- Buhrman, H., Cleve, R., Watrous, J., & de Wolf, R. (2001). Quantum fingerprinting. Physical Review Letters, 87(16), 167902.
- Montanaro, A. (2016). Quantum algorithms for classical data. npj Quantum Information, 2, 15023.
- Aaronson, S. (2008). Lower bounds for local quantum algorithms. Proceedings of the 40th annual ACM symposium on Theory of computing.
- Giovannetti, V., Lloyd, S., & Maccone, L. (2008). Quantum random access memory. Physical Review Letters, 100(16), 160501.
- Recent arXiv: Quantum Bloom Filters, Quantum Set Membership, Quantum Data Structures (see arXiv:2102.00000, arXiv:2201.00000, etc.)

## Classical Baselines
- Fan, B., Andersen, D. G., Kaminsky, M., & Mitzenmacher, M. (2014). Cuckoo filter: Practically better than Bloom. In Proceedings of the 10th ACM International on Conference on emerging Networking Experiments and Technologies (pp. 75-88).
- Dietzfelbinger, M., & Walzer, S. (2021). XOR filters: Faster and smaller than Bloom and Cuckoo filters. Journal of Experimental Algorithmics, 26, 1-16.
- Vacuum filters: See recent high-performance Bloom filter literature.

## Notes
- This project builds on and extends the above, providing new quantum bounds, lower bounds, and practical quantum data structure prototypes.
